Hi, my name is Ottó Ingi and this is my website. I ‘m 24 years old Industrial Engineering student and salesperson. I live in Garðabær with my fiancée. My plan is to graduate from the University of Iceland in June 2020 and go on to continuing education to get a master’s degree in engineering. I work with school in Glerverksmiðjan Samverk so I can save up money and pay my bills. My hobbies are school, friends, sports and traveling. I always try to do my best in everything i take on and I think my competitiveness drives my ambition to do great things in the future.
